References

The Seraph Safety Mission whitepaper and public materials are grounded in Arkansas child fatality review reporting, national surveillance (CDC / NCHS), CPSC safety information, peer-reviewed evidence on bathtub submersion incidents, and applicable federal consumer product safety standards.

Note: Some evidence on supervision-lapse patterns comes from peer-reviewed data outside Arkansas. We use it to inform program design and messaging, while Arkansas ICDR reporting anchors the state context.
