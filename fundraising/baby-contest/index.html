Precious Baby Contest

Show off your adorable baby and help save lives! Enter your child's photo, share with friends and family, and rally votes. Every vote is a donation that funds bath safety education for Arkansas families.

Contest Rules

  • Open to babies ages 0–3 years
  • One entry per child
  • Photos must be family-appropriate
  • Entries reviewed within 48 hours
  • Voting period announced when gallery opens
  • Each $1 donated = 1 vote
  • All proceeds benefit Seraph Safety Mission

Prizes

  • 1st Place: Custom baby gift basket + Seraph Safety Wrap
  • 2nd Place: Gift card + Seraph Safety Wrap
  • 3rd Place: Seraph Safety Wrap + certificate
  • All Entrants: Bath safety kit + participation certificate
All votes are donations. Seraph Safety Mission is a 501(c)(3) — your votes are tax-deductible (EIN: 41-3868771).
