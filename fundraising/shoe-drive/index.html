Shoe Drive for Safety

Your gently used shoes get a second life — and fund bath safety education for Arkansas families. Clean out your closets for a cause that saves lives.

Find a Drop-Off Host a Collection

How It Works

1.

Collect Shoes

Gather gently used shoes from your home, office, church, or school. All sizes and styles welcome!

2.

Drop Off or Ship

Bring them to a drop-off location or contact us for shipping details.

3.

Shoes Become Safety

Shoes are collected, sorted, and recycled through partner programs. The funds go directly to our mission.

What We Accept

We Accept

  • Gently used shoes (all sizes)
  • New shoes
  • Athletic shoes, boots, sandals, dress shoes
  • Children's, men's, and women's shoes
  • Paired shoes (tied or rubber-banded together)

We Cannot Accept

  • Heavily worn or damaged shoes
  • Single shoes (no match)
  • Wet or moldy shoes
  • Roller skates, ice skates, or cleats
