About Seraph Safety Mission

Seraph Safety Mission is organized exclusively for charitable, educational, and scientific purposes under Section 501(c)(3).

Mission

To provide drowning prevention education and safety tools to all Arkansas babies in the form of the Seraph Safety Wrap.

Tagline: “Guarding Our most Precious Splashes.”

Governance note

Our work prioritizes public benefit: distributing prevention education and, where feasible, safety tools for families. Any product development or manufacturing activity should be kept separate from charitable programming with independent oversight and fair-market transactions.
